Training


At Waste Aside, we are dedicated to empowering individuals and organizations through our comprehensive training services. By equipping participants with the knowledge and skills needed for sustainable waste management practices, we help create a greener and more environmentally conscious future.

Our training services include:

  • - Workshops on waste reduction, recycling, and resource recovery techniques.

  • - Hands-on training for using recycling equipment and machinery efficiently.

  • - Educational programs for schools and communities to promote environmental awareness.
